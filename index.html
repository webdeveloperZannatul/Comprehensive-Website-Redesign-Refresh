> Reliance Care Solutions was founded in Toledo with a simple mission: deliver high-quality, compassionate support services that honor the dignity of every individual and strengthen families.

We specialize in structured programs for children, youth, and adults with intellectual and developmental disabilities. Our team of licensed professionals works hand-in-hand with families to create personalized plans that promote independence, growth, and joy.
